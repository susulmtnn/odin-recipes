Lentil soup

Description

This hearty meal will comfort you during the cold winter months.

The best part of this dish is that it is also vegan friendly!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 dl of dry red or yellow lentils
  • 400 gr of spicy tomato sauce
  • 400 gr of natural tomato sauce
  • Can of coconut cream
  • 9 dl of water
  • butter
  • salt
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teascoon of cajun pepper mix
  • 2 teaspoons of ginger powder

Steps

  1. Fry the minced onion and garlic in butter in a big pot with the spices until onion is soft
  2. Add the tomato sauces and water. Boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the washed lentils and boil for 40 minutes.
  4. Taste the soup and add salt if needed.
  5. Move the pot off the heat and add the coconut cream.
  6. The soup is ready!
