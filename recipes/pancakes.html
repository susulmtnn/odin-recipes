Pancakes

Description

This recipe is the heart of a party! Invite all your friends over and cook together.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 dl of flour
  • 6 dl of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Oil or butter to cook
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar, if desired

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients in a bowl with a whisk and let sit in fridge for 30min - 1 hour.
  2. Add a big spoonful of dough on a hot pan.
  3. Wait until you are able to turn a pancake and fry the other side.
  4. Plate the pancake and add the desired toppings! Bon app√©tit!
